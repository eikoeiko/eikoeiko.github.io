Eiko's first large project was his film persona, expressed through his YouTube channel. He has been uploading since 2015.

In the winter of 2016, Eiko started a website service with friends called Developster.io. The goal was to connect creative teenagers worldwide and help them collaborate and build their skillset.
In the summer of 2017, Developster became unsustainable and shut down, but remains a massive learning experience, a substantial use of his school's student fund.

Eiko now works as a freelance graphic designer, focussing on web, apps, and brands. Photographs, films, fashion and sketches are his main artistic focus today.

Eiko wrote his biography in the third person, so as to appear more esteemed then he is.
