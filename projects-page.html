My first large project was my film persona, expressed through my YouTube channel. I have been uploading since 2015.

In the winter of 2016, me some teenage peers co-founded a website service with called Developster.io. Developster's goal was to bring together teenagers who were making things and help them collaborate and upskill
In the summer of 2017, Developster became unsustainable and shut down, but remains a massive learning experience, and a good use of my school's student fund.

I now works as a freelance graphic designer, focussing on web, apps, and brands. Photographs, films, clothing and marketing are my main artistic focus today.
